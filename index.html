About me

My name is Giuseppe D'Orazio and I am a computer science student at York University. I've been interested in technology and how it functions ever since I was young. I started programming in high school and have been learning ever since.

Why does this site look like this?

The visual aesthetic of this website was inspired from webpages I discovered while browsing Neocities. I wanted to create a similar retro feel with bright colors and simple layouts. Despite not being around for the early days of the internet, I find the simplicity and charm of these designs appealing.

I also find something to be admirable about the individuality of the old web. Each page was unique, reflecting the personality of its creator. This website is a small homage to that era, where creativity and self-expression was more commonplace about the internet. It's my way of combatting the sameness of modern websites and social media platforms.
